After opening the Library file, go to the top left corner and click on File.
Navigate to Desktop\Library-master\Library-master\LibrarySaveFile.txt and click open.
The library has now been loaded.
You may now enter a new book, create a new user, checkout a book, return a book,
Look at the books that are currently checked out and which patron has those books. 
You may also look to see what books there are available.
When finished with the library please click back on the File button and choose Quit.
This will save all current information to the database.
